Want to see your content in the viewer?

Consult the Timeline Instructions (pdf) and the example Excel spreadsheet (97-2003, .xls format), that you can use to prepare your content for the timeline viewer. Please remember to keep content simple: use single(') instead of double quotes("), use "first" instead of 1st, and so on. These cause problems.

If you don't have Excel, check out Open Office as a free alternative. E-mail your file to tedtower1@yahoo.com. I'll add your content.
